"Spicy Honey Ramen Hack"


   Total Time: 15 Mins   Serves: 1


Ingredients:

  • Instant Ramen (1 pack)
  • Water or broth (vegtable, beef, or chicken): 2 cups (500 ml)
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp hot sauce or saratcha (adjust to taste)
  • Boiled egg (optional)
  • Sesame seeds (optional garnish)
  • Green onion (optional garnish)

    • Instructions:

  • Cook the Ramen:  Follow the instructions on the package, boiling the noodles as directed.
  • Drain the broth:  Once the noodles are ready, drain about half of the broth to keep the dish flavorful but not too soupy.
  • Mix the sauce: Stir in 1 tablespoon of honey and 1 teaspoon of hot sauce. Adjust the spiciness based on your preference.
  • Add extra toppings:  For more texture and flavor, top the ramen with a soft-boiled egg.
  • Garnish and serve:Garnish and serve: Sprinkle seasme seeds and chopped green onions (if available).
  • Serve hot and enjoy!

    • Notes:

  • To serve more people, simply scale up the ingredients accordingly on the package, boiling the noodles as directed.
    •      (e.g., for 2 servings,use 2 packs of ramen, 2 tbsp honey,and 2 tsp hot sauce).
